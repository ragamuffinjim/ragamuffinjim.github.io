My digital garden

So for several months now I have wandering the innertubez searching for the perfect "blogging" platform to use to put my writings online. I am not satisfied with any of them. People are beginning to laugh at me for always moving around and changing where I post stuff. I bet some folk have ten rss feeds all related to some idiotic thing that I have tried in the last couple of months.

I think that I have finally figured out that I don't want to "blog" as much as I want a place online for me to be me, a place where I can experiment with stuff, maybe do some coding, maybe do some blogging. I am not sure what all will go in this digital garden but I am pretty sure that it will be a seeming incoherent mess to someone who is not walking around in my head.
